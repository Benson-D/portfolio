About Me

Hello my name is Dan, I'm a full-stack software engineer. I strive to create programs that make people's lives easier and more efficient. Previously I was a classical musician in percussion. I performed around the midwest playing in various orchestras and teaching students how to play percussion. As a musician, I love having the opportunity to use my creativity to solve complex tech problems that require thinking out of the box. Music is constantly changing in regards to genres, compositions, and playing techniques. The idea of constantly learning is always in effect with music, which really helped me gravitate towards the tech world.

I'm open to explore different opportunities, so feel free to contact me! For more information please view my resume.
